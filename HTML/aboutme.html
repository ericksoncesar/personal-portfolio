About Me!

Hi! I'm Erickson, but I go by Cesar. I'm a software engineer with a strong passion for coding, design, and content creation. My journey started at 17 when I began teaching myself to code, driven by curiosity and a desire to create something meaningful. Since then, I’ve been constantly experimenting with new technologies and challenging myself to grow.

In addition to my work in tech, I'm into lifting weights, which keeps me energized and motivated. When I'm not coding, you’ll often find me reading, whether it’s fiction to unwind or tech books to keep learning. I also enjoy diving into the world of design, UI/UX, where I combine my creative side with my technical skills to create user-friendly, visually appealing experiences.

Through my content creation, I share insights into the tech world, personal projects, and the importance of maintaining a balance between work, fitness, school, and creativity. I’m excited to continue building, learning, and growing, and I hope to inspire others to do the same!
